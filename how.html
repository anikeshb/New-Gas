How do I switch to natural gas?

Are you ready to make the switch to clean, efficient and convenient natural gas? Switching to natural gas is easier than you think and Eversource is here to guide you through the natural gas conversion process.

“ I like that I only pay for my actual usage and don’t have to pay upfront…”

We can answer your questions and make the switch to natural gas as easy as possible. Below are several easy steps to guide you through the process.

  • Find out if natural gas is on your street using our online tool.

  • Call us at 855-645-2427 (855-OIL2GAS) or email us at insidesales@eversource.com.

  • We will provide you with all of the information you need, including any required forms.

  • Find a contractor to help you verify and choose the natural gas equipment that is adequate for your household and your needs

  • Eversource will schedule your service line installation, set your meter and activate your gas service.

If you are already using natural gas in your home and you switch to a natural gas heating system, call us at 800-989-0900 to ensure your rate is changed to the lower gas heating rate.
